ABOUT US

Succeed Youth Association Mutungo is a fully registered community-based organization committed to making education, good health, clean water, and the safety of children a fundamental right. Our association collaborates with organizations, churches, individual volunteers, and entities with shared goals to ensure that children from the most economically disadvantaged families have equal access to education.

Founded with a passion for positive change, Succeed Youth Association operates with a vision to create a world where every child, regardless of socio-economic background, has the opportunity to thrive. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to foster partnerships with schools, communities, and individuals who share our belief in the transformative power of education.

At Succeed Youth Association, we understand that education is not only a key to individual success but also a catalyst for community development. By collaborating with local schools and volunteers, we strive to break down barriers to education, empower vulnerable children, and build a future where every young mind can reach its full potential. Join us on our journey to make a lasting impact on the lives of children and communities we serve.

Location

Succeed Youth Association is situated in Mutungo, a vibrant community in the heart of Uganda.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading force in empowering the youth, creating academically competitive individuals, and nurturing socially responsible leaders within Uganda.

Mission

Our mission is to raise a successful and God-fearing generation by providing quality education, promoting skill development, and instilling values that contribute to the holistic growth of every child we serve.

Cultural Practise

Our schools respect all people from different cultures and tribes. We also have pupils from different regions of the country with different nationalities and tribes like:

  • Baganda
  • Itesot
  • Banyankore
  • Banyarwanda
  • Langi

Practical Lessons

Our schools deliver practical lessons which are learner centred and involve active participation of pupils.

The learner centred approach helps pupils to grasp and master the content delivered to them.

Educational Tours

Our schools organise educational tours and trips to different places of the country like:

  • Rwenzori Moutains National Park
  • Queen Elizabeth National Park
  • Kitagata Hotsprings

Such educational tours help pupils relate what they study in class with the real world.

Lessons

We also conduct P.E practical lessons at our school in order to equip our learners with sports skills which they need to apply in their day-to-day life because the world is changing so fast with new merging sports every day!

We teach, among others, the following programs:

  • Volley Ball
  • Netball
  • Football
